## Subnets





### Subnetting Definition:



Process of breaking down a network into smaller and more manageable networks.



### Why organizations split networks into smaller sections:



Organizations use subnetting for many reasons. They could divide the networks to enhance security and increase efficiency in the network. They could also split the networks to decrease congestion across different regions. Lastly, they can use subnetting as their company grows so they don't have to completely change the current network.
